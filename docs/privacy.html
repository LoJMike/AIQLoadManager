Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 27, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 27, 2026

Short version: Your prompts, AI responses, and API keys never leave your machine. We collect only anonymous usage analytics (opt-out) and the minimum billing information needed to process your subscription.

1. Who We Are

AI Queue Load Manager ("we," "us," "our") is a desktop software product operated by Michael Hall / conxion visual communications. If you have privacy questions, contact us at mike@conxion.biz.

2. What Data We Collect

Data type What it is Why we collect it
Billing information Name, email address, payment details To process your subscription. Handled by Paddle (our payment processor) — we do not store card numbers.
Anonymous analytics Feature usage events, crash reports, app version, OS type To understand how the Software is used and identify bugs. Collected via PostHog. Contains no personally identifiable information. Opt-out available in app settings.
Support communications Emails or messages you send us To respond to your support request.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

The following data stays entirely on your machine and is never transmitted to us:

  • Your prompts and AI responses — all API calls go directly from your machine to the AI provider. They do not pass through our servers.
  • Your API keys — stored locally using encrypted, OS-backed secure storage (electron-store). We never see or receive your API keys.
  • Your queue history and usage stats — stored in a local SQLite database on your device.
  • Your conversation content — conversation history is held in memory only and is never written to any server.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect only to:

  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Send transactional emails (receipts, renewal notices, important product updates)
  • Improve the Software based on anonymous usage patterns
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

Paddle — Our payment processor and Merchant of Record. Paddle processes your payment and issues your invoice. Their privacy policy applies to data they collect: paddle.com/legal/privacy.

PostHog — Anonymous analytics. PostHog receives event data with no name, email, or identifiable information attached. You can opt out in the app's Settings panel. PostHog privacy policy: posthog.com/privacy.

AI providers — When you use the Software to send requests to Claude, OpenAI, Gemini, Groq, DeepSeek, Mistral, or xAI Grok, those requests are governed by each provider's own privacy policy. We have no control over and are not responsible for how those providers handle your data.

6. Data Retention

Billing records are retained as required for tax and legal compliance (typically 7 years). Anonymous analytics data is retained per PostHog's standard retention settings. Support communications are retained for up to 2 years. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at mike@conxion.biz. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are in the European Economic Area or the UK, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal data in our possession. Your API keys and local application data are protected by OS-level encryption on your device. Payment data is handled entirely by Paddle and is never stored on our systems.

9. Children's Privacy

The Software is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or via a notice in the Software. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: mike@conxion.biz
