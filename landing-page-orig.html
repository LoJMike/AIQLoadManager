Frequently asked questions

Do I need to provide my own AI API keys?

Yes. AI Queue Manager connects to the APIs you already have — it doesn't mark up or resell AI access. You pay providers directly at their published rates, and the app helps you use them efficiently.

What's the difference between monthly and lifetime pricing?

Monthly gives you access for as long as you're subscribed and includes all future updates in that tier. Lifetime is a one-time payment for unlimited use — typically breaking even vs. monthly in 7–8 months.

Can I really use it completely free?

Yes. The Free tier is genuinely usable — especially if you use Gemini, Groq, or Mistral, which all have free usage tiers. You can queue and route prompts at no cost indefinitely.

Is my data safe? Does anything leave my computer?

Your prompts and responses go directly from your machine to the AI provider's API — nothing passes through our servers. API keys are stored in your OS credential store (Windows Credential Manager / macOS Keychain).

Can I upgrade or downgrade later?

Yes. Monthly subscribers can upgrade or downgrade any time and only pay the difference. Lifetime purchases are per-tier — upgrading to a higher tier is the price difference between the two tiers.

What platforms does it support?

Windows 10 and 11, and macOS. Both get the same features. Linux builds are not currently offered but may be added in a future release.
